#**Privacy Policy**

Thumbs Up! takes your privacy seriously. I provide this privacy policy to notify you that your personal information is not used, stored or transmitted by Thumbs Up!. The function of the Thumbs Up! is to display exercise videos to the user with an optional local alert which can be allowed or disallowed by the user. The local alert is used as a daily reminder to use the app.

##Reminder notification
Thumbs Up! uses the UNUserNotificationCenter object to manage the notification-related behavior. You will be given the choice to allow the app to provide you with a local alert in accordance with the way user interaction works on Apple devices.

I reserve the right to update or change my Privacy Policy at any time and you should check this Privacy Policy periodically. If I make any material changes to this Privacy Policy, I will notify you either through the email address you have provided me, or by placing a prominent notice on my app's website.
